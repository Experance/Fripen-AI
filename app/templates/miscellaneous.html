Why are we making this


    Help the Farming Industry
  • A reduction in human labor, increasing efficiency
  • The speed of identifying increases

    • Money Saved and more Safety
  • More robot work can over time save millions of dollars
  • Having AI and robots to do repetitive work such as picking ripe fruit can also reduce the possibility of injuries, like when someone falls off a ladder, or heat exhaustion when picking fruit when it is burning hot outside. A robot with this AI can save time and money.

Type of ML Problem


    Supervised Learning
  • Fruits are given labels(such as ripe or unripe)
  • Then the model learns to determine which class an observation belongs in.
