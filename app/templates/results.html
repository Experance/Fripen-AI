What are some challenges of Computer Vision?

Computer Vision is very limited. The computer takes in an image as a bunch of numbers and it's job is to identify patterns; The model needs to be conscious and have a lot of context in order to identify things, we need to continuously monitor the model's progress. But take the context of said model and compare it to that of the human brain, a lifetime of continuously training and taking input versus our model, which is a small fraction compared to the human brain.
